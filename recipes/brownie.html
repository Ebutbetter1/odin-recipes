Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray a baking pan (8x8 recommended) and line it with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the butter, then add the sugar and cocoa powder. Whisk for 30 seconds, then whisk in the eggs, vanilla extract, and salt.
  3. Add the flour and chocolate chips and mix together with a spatula until just combined. Spread the batter into the pan.
  4. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes. Let the brownies cool completely before slicing.
