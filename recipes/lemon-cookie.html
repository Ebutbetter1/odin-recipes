Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon lemon extract
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 3/4 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 drop yellow food coloring (optional)

Steps

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl.
  2. With an electric mixer, beat together the sugar and butter until creamy. Add in egg, vanilla extract, lemon extract, lemon zest, lemon juice, and yellow food coloring. Once combined, add in the flour mixture and beat until just combined.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  5. Use a cookie scoop to scoop out portions of dough. Flatten them to about 1/2-inch thickness.
  6. Place confectioners' sugar into a shallow bowl and coat the flattened portions of dough thoroughly. Place them onto the baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes or until the cookies are no longer glossy and have a matte look. Let them cool completely.
